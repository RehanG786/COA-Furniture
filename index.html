About COA Furniture Studio

Founded in 2025 in Mumbai, India, COA Furniture Studio was built on a simple idea: create honest furniture that brings clarity, calm, and character into everyday spaces.

Our work blends timeless design principles with natural materials, emphasizing balance, proportion, and silence. Every piece is made with purpose — slow-crafted, detail-led, and designed to last.

At COA, we believe great furniture doesn’t shout. It shapes the room, quietly.

Details That Matter

Solid Wood

We use responsibly sourced, full-grain hardwood — no MDF, no shortcuts.

Hand-Finished

Each piece is finished by hand for depth, texture, and timeless character.

Proportional Design

We design for human scale — every curve and line meant to feel right.

Sustainable Practice

From materials to packaging, we minimize waste and maximize purpose.
